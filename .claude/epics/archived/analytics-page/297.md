### SVG Coordinate Math
- viewBox: `0 0 400 250` (or similar)
- Chart area: padding for labels and axis
- Bar width: fixed (e.g., 60px), centered in each section
- Bar height: `(win_rate / 100) * chartHeight`
- Y-axis origin at bottom (SVG y increases downward, so bar y = chartTop + chartHeight - barHeight)
