## Description

Two small hardening items: (1) Reject negative TTL values in `ServiceCache.set()` with
a `ValueError`. (2) Verify that existing `math.isfinite()` guards in `scoring/normalization.py`
handle fully-NaN indicator series gracefully, and add explicit test cases to prove it.
