### Edge Cases
- Contract with `bid=0, ask=0` (zero mid) → spread component = 0.0
- Contract with `spread > max_spread_pct * mid` → clamped to 0.0
- Contract with `OI = 1` → log10(2)/4 ≈ 0.075 (low but non-zero)
- All candidates identical liquidity → raw delta ordering preserved
